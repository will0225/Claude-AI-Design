Q2 2026 Client Performance Report — Northwind Digital (retainer client)

Scope: Reviewed Google Analytics, Meta Ads, and email campaign data for April–June 2026.

Key metrics:
- Website sessions: 48,200 (+12% vs Q1)
- Paid social ROAS: 3.2x (target was 2.8x)
- Email open rate: 34% (industry avg ~21%)
- Top landing page: /product-launch — 8,400 sessions, 6.1% conversion
- Underperformer: /blog/resources — high traffic, 0.4% conversion

Findings:
1. Product launch campaign exceeded ROAS target; recommend increasing budget 15% in Q3.
2. Blog traffic is strong but CTAs are buried below fold on mobile.
3. Email segment "inactive 90 days" has 2,100 contacts — win-back campaign untapped.
4. Meta creative fatigue on ad set "Summer Promo A" — CTR dropped 40% over 6 weeks.

Recommendations:
- Reallocate $2,000/mo from underperforming display to Meta retargeting.
- A/B test sticky mobile CTA on top 5 blog posts.
- Launch 3-email win-back sequence in July.
- Refresh Summer Promo A creative before August push.

Notes:
- Client asked for more detail on competitor benchmarking — not in scope this quarter.
- Legal approved use of aggregated data in external reports.
